More Info:
Aqua-E® is a unique, patented ** product which supplies the complete Vitamin E family of tocopherols plus tocotrienols in water soluble form. It is particularly rich in gamma-tocopherol and tocotrienols.
Aqua-E® is specially formulated for enhanced absorption especially for people who have conditions associated with malabsorption. Aqua-E® is odorless and has a neutral taste. It mixes easily with most beverages and water. Dosing may be easily adjusted to meet specific needs. It is also particularly suitable for people who have difficulty swallowing capsules and tablets and those who are strict vegetarians.
